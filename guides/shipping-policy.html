Shipping Policy

How we pack, ship and deliver your gemstones.

Dispatch Time

Most ready items are dispatched within 3–5 working days after order confirmation.

Delivery Time

Delivery usually takes 3–10 working days within India, depending on your location and courier service.

Shipping Charges

Shipping is calculated based on weight, value and destination. Final charges are confirmed with you during order confirmation.

Packaging

All gemstones are securely packed with cushioning and, where required, tamper-proof packing.
