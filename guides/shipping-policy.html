Shipping Policy

1. Order Processing Time

Orders are processed and dispatched within 1–3 business days from our warehouse (unless otherwise mentioned on product page). You will receive a dispatch confirmation email / WhatsApp with tracking details once your order is shipped.

2. Domestic Shipping (India)

  • We ship via reputed courier / parcel services across India.
  • Standard shipping charge: ₹100 – ₹500 depending on weight and location. Free shipping on orders above ₹ **[YOUR FREE-SHIP MINIMUM]** (if applicable).
  • Typical delivery time: 4–10 business days depending on region and courier performance.
  • Packing: We ensure secure packaging using bubble wrap & strong boxes to protect gemstones — but natural stones may have minor inclusions or natural marks; these are not defects.‍

3. International Shipping

  • We ship worldwide via reputable international courier services (DHL, FedEx, etc.).
  • Shipping charges depend on weight, destination country and declared value; charges are automatically calculated at checkout.
  • Estimated delivery time: 7–21 business days from dispatch (may vary due to customs / local courier delays).
  • Import duties, customs fees or local taxes (if any) are borne by the buyer in their country.
  • We declare accurate product value and HS code but cannot control customs regulations or delays once package leaves India.

4. Order Tracking

Once dispatched, you will receive a tracking number via email / WhatsApp. Please use that number to track your order until delivery. If tracking does not update for more than 5 business days (domestic) or 10 business days (international), contact us — we will liaise with courier or initiate support.

5. Lost or Damaged Shipments

- If your package is delivered damaged, do not discard the packaging. - Take clear photos of the packaging + damage + product within 48 hours and contact us. - We will verify and — if verified — offer **replacement or full refund**. - For international orders, customs clearance or delays are outside our control; we will assist but cannot guarantee compensation for customs-related losses.
