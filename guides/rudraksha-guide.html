Rudraksha Guide

Understand different mukhi Rudraksha, their energies & how to wear them.

What is Rudraksha?

Rudraksha is the sacred seed of the Rudraksha tree, associated with Lord Shiva. Each bead has natural lines or faces called “mukhi”. Every mukhi carries a specific vibration and planetary connection.

General Benefits

  • Balances mind, emotions and nervous system
  • Supports meditation and spiritual growth
  • Protection from negative energies and psychic attacks
  • Helps in stabilising over-thinking and anxiety

How to Wear Rudraksha

  1. Choose authentic, uncracked beads from a trusted source.
  2. Monday or auspicious Shiva day is best for wearing.
  3. Clean with water, apply tilak, and energize with “Om Namah Shivaya” or specific beeja mantra.
  4. Wear close to skin — mala or bracelet — and avoid touching with dirty hands.
Note: High mukhi (10–21) Rudraksha should only be worn after expert consultation.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I wear Rudraksha while sleeping or bathing?

Ideally remove during bath (especially using chemical soaps) and intimate moments. For sleep it is optional, depending on comfort.

Is Rudraksha only for Shiva devotees?

No, Rudraksha works as an energy tool and can be worn by anyone respectfully, irrespective of religion.
