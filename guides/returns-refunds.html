Returns & Refunds Policy

1. What we accept

We offer returns or refunds only for the following valid cases:

  • Wrong product sent (different item than ordered).
  • Product damaged in transit — cracked stones, broken jewelry, missing components.
  • Shipment lost by courier (only if confirmed by courier company).

2. What we do not accept

As gemstones and Rudraksha are natural items, the following are not eligible for return:

  • Minor natural inclusions, internal lines, surface texture or colour variation (common in natural stones).
  • Discolouration or scratches due to misuse, harsh chemicals, salt-water immersion or improper cleaning.
  • Change of mind — e.g. you don’t like colour or changed your decision after wearing.
  • Any custom-made jewelry or gemstone settings specially ordered.

3. Return / Refund Procedure

  1. Send us an email / WhatsApp within 7 days of delivery with order number, photos of product and packaging.
  2. If issue qualifies, we will share a return label or address depending on your location.
  3. Return item in the same original packaging, unused and unaltered.
  4. Once we receive and inspect, refund will be processed within 5 business days (for domestic) or equivalent value/courier credit (for international, depending on shipping status).

4. Refund Method

Refunds are processed to the original payment method or account — depending on what was used during purchase (UPI, bank transfer, etc.). For COD orders, refund may be via bank transfer or store credit depending on agreement.

5. Shipping & Return Charges

Return shipping cost is borne by the customer, unless the return is due to our error (wrong item or damage). In such cases, we will provide prepaid return shipping.

6. Contact for Returns

Contact us at WhatsApp / Email / Contact Us page with your order ID — we will guide you through the return steps.
