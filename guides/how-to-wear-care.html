How to Wear & Care

The complete guide to wearing, energizing and maintaining Gemstones & Rudraksha

How to Wear Gemstones

Gemstones are energetic amplifiers. For astrological benefits, they must be energized and worn correctly based on planet, metal, finger and weight.

Best Day & Time

  • Sunday – Ruby (Sun)
  • Monday – Pearl (Moon)
  • Tuesday – Red Coral (Mars)
  • Wednesday – Emerald (Mercury)
  • Thursday – Yellow Sapphire (Jupiter)
  • Friday – Diamond / Opal (Venus)
  • Saturday – Blue Sapphire / Hessonite / Cat’s Eye (Saturn / Rahu / Ketu)

Which Finger?

  • Ring finger – Yellow Sapphire, Ruby, Diamond
  • Middlfinger – Blue Sapphire, Amethyst
  • Little finger – Emerald, Pearl
  • Index finger – Red Coral, Hessonite (conditional)

Step-by-Step Energizing Method

  1. Place gemstone in a clean bowl with Ganga jal + raw milk + honey + curd + pure water (optional mix).
  2. Rinse gently and wipe with a clean cloth.
  3. Place on a copper or silver plate facing sunlight or diya.
  4. Chant 108 times: “Om Hreem Namah” or related planetary mantra.

How to Cleanse Gemstones

  • Running natural water
  • Incense smoke or dhoop
  • Moonlight bath (Full Moon is ideal)
  • Sound cleansing (Tibetan bowl / Om chanting)
  • Salt cleansing (Avoid for Pearl, Coral, Opal & Emerald)

How to Wear Rudraksha

  1. Best day: Monday or Pradosh.
  2. Clean with water, apply sandalwood paste.
  3. Chant 108 times: Om Namah Shivaya
  4. Wear close to the skin (mala / bracelet).
  5. Avoid wearing during bath with chemical soaps.
Higher mukhis (10–21) & powerful combos should be worn only under expert guidance.

Do & Don’ts

Do

  • Wear gemstone touching the skin
  • Remove gemstone during surgery or radiation
  • Clean monthly for best results
  • Use mantra & intention

Don’t

  • Do not exchange gemstones worn by others
  • Do not mix too many strong stones together
  • Do not use salt water on soft stones
  • Do not wear Rudraksha in intimate or violent situations
