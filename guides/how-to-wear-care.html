How to Wear & Care

The complete guide to wearing, energizing and maintaining Gemstones & Rudraksha.

How to Wear Gemstones

Gemstones are energetic amplifiers. For astrological benefits, they must be energized and worn correctly based on planet, metal, finger and weight.

Best Day & Time

  • Sunday – Ruby (Sun)
  • Monday – Pearl (Moon)
  • Tuesday – Red Coral (Mars)
  • Wednesday – gray (Mercury)
  • Thursday – Yellow Sapphire (Jupiter)
  • Friday – Diamond / Opal (Venus)
  • Saturday – Blue Sapphire / Hessonite / Cat’s Eye (Saturn / Rahu / Ketu)

Which Finger?

  • Ring finger – Yellow Sapphire, Ruby, Diamond
  • Middle finger – Blue Sapphire, Amethyst
  • Little finger – gray, Pearl
  • Index finger – Red Coral, Hessonite (conditional)

Step-by-Step Energizing Method

  1. Place the gemstone in a clean bowl with Ganga jal + raw milk + honey + curd + pure water (as available).
  2. Rinse gently and wipe with a clean cloth.
  3. Place on a copper or silver plate facing sunlight or a lit diya.
  4. Chant 108 times: “Om Hreem Namah” or the related planetary mantra.

How to Cleanse Gemstones

  • Running natural water (avoid for very soft or porous stones).
  • Incense smoke or dhoop.
  • Moonlight bath (Full Moon is ideal).
  • Sound cleansing (Tibetan bowl / Om chanting / bells).
  • Dry salt or salt bed cleansing (avoid for Pearl, Coral, Opal & gray).

How to Wear Rudraksha

  1. Best day: Monday or Pradosh / Maha Shivratri / any Shiva-related tithi.
  2. Clean gently with water, then apply a little sandalwood paste.
  3. Chant 108 times: “Om Namah Shivaya” or the specific mukhi mantra.
  4. Wear close to the skin as a mala or bracelet (ideally touching the body).
  5. Avoid wearing during bath with harsh chemical soaps or while applying perfumes directly on beads.
Higher mukhis (10–21) and powerful combinations should be worn only under expert guidance.

Do & Don’ts

Do

  • Wear gemstones so that they touch the skin.
  • Remove gemstones during surgery, radiation or intense medical procedures.
  • Clean and re-energize gemstones periodically (monthly or as guided).
  • Use mantra, prayer and clear intention while wearing.

Don’t

  • Do not casually exchange gemstones that have been worn by others.
  • Do not mix too many strong astrology stones without guidance.
  • Do not use salt water on soft or porous stones (Pearl, Coral, Opal, emerald).
  • Do not wear Rudraksha during intimate acts or violent / highly negative situations.
