Frequently Asked Questions

Ordering, shipping, payments & authenticity of our gemstones.

Ordering

How do I place an order?

You can add products to your cart from the “Buy Online” section and then complete your details on the checkout page.

Can I order through WhatsApp?

Yes, you can contact us on WhatsApp via the floating button and we can assist with selection & ordering.

Shipping

Do you ship across India?

Yes, we ship to most locations in India. Exact shipping charges are confirmed after order based on weight & destination.

Do you ship internationally?

International shipping may be available on request. Contact us directly for options & charges.

Payments & Currencies

Which payment methods do you accept?

Currently we support COD confirmation, UPI and online transfers. Gateway/card options will be enabled soon.

Which currency are prices shown in?

All prices on the website are shown in Indian Rupees (INR).

Authenticity & Certification

Are your gemstones natural?

We focus on natural gemstones. If any treatment is present (like heating), it will be clearly mentioned.

Do you provide certificates?

Certification can be arranged through reputed labs on request, especially for high-value stones.
