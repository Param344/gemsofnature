Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Are your gemstones & Rudraksha 100% natural and genuine?

Yes. We source all items from trusted miners, workshops and suppliers. Each piece is inspected for authenticity, clarity and structural integrity. For high-value stones, we provide lab-certificates when available. We do not sell synthetic or imitation gemstones in our “Natural / Healing / Rudraksha” categories.

How do I choose the right gemstone for me?

It depends on your intention (healing, protection, prosperity, chakra balance), and if you follow Vedic astrology — your birth chart. We recommend reading our How to Choose a Gemstone guide for detailed instructions. For strong stones (like Blue Sapphire, Cat’s Eye, Hessonite etc.) — consult an astrologer before purchase.

Can I wear multiple stones or Rudraksha together?

Yes — for healing, chakra, and spiritual use, combining gentle stones (like Clear Quartz, Rose Quartz, Amethyst) with Rudraksha is usually safe. For pure astrological gemstones and high-energy Rudraksha (strong mukhi), mixing many together is not recommended unless advised by a qualified astrologer or spiritual guide.

How long will my order take to reach me?

Delivery time depends on your location and shipping method. Domestic orders within India typically dispatch within 1–3 working days and reach in 4–10 days depending on courier/town. International orders dispatch in 1–4 working days; delivery time depends on destination country, customs clearance and courier service — it usually takes 7–21 days after dispatch.

Do I get a refund if I’m not satisfied or if the product is damaged?

We allow returns/refunds under certain conditions (see our Returns & Refunds Policy). Please inspect your order within 7 days of delivery and inform us with clear photos if there is damage, defect or wrong item. For natural stones, minor inclusions or colour variation — mentioned in description — are not considered defects.

Do you ship internationally? What are shipping charges and taxes?

Yes, we ship worldwide. Shipping charges depend on weight, value, destination and courier. International orders may attract import duties, customs fees or taxes in the destination country — these are borne by the customer and are not included in product price or our shipping charges.

How should I care for gemstones and Rudraksha?

Gently clean gemstones with soft cloth; avoid harsh chemicals or salt-water for soft / porous stones (like Pearl, Coral, Opal, Malachite). Rudraksha should be kept dry, cleaned occasionally, and oiled lightly to prevent cracking. For detailed care instructions, see our How to Wear & Care page.
