Gemstone Cleansing & Energizing

Purify your gemstones to restore vibration, clarity & spiritual power.

Why cleansing is important?

Gemstones absorb energies from people and environments. Cleansing removes unwanted vibrations and restores the natural frequency of the stone to work effectively.

Methods of Cleansing

  • Running natural water
  • Himalayan salt water (not for soft stones like Pearl, Coral, Emerald)
  • Incense smoke / Dhoop
  • Moonlight (Purnima is ideal)
  • Mantra chanting

Energizing Mantras

Recite “Om Hreem Namah” 108 times while holding the stone or keeping it on a copper plate.

Frequently Asked Questions

How often should gemstones be cleansed?

Ideally once a month or after emotionally heavy situations.

Can salt damage gemstones?

Yes, do not use salt water on soft stones like Pearl, Coral, Emerald or Opal.
