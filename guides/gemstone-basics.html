Gemstone Basics

Understand natural gemstones, treatments, certification & purpose.

What are Natural Gemstones?

Natural gemstones are minerals formed beneath the Earth over millions of years. Their unique molecular structure produces healing vibrations and visual beauty. Each gemstone carries special metaphysical and astrological significance.

Gemstone Types

  • Precious stones : Diamond, Ruby, Sapphire, Emerald
  • Semi-precious stones : Amethyst, Citrine, Opal, Garnet, Peridot, Tourmaline etc.
  • Organic natural materials : Pearl, Coral, Amber

Natural vs Treated vs Synthetic

Category Meaning Effect in Astrology
Natural Pure, unchanged stone from nature Strongest positive impact
Treated Heated / filled for color enhancement Medium effect, depends on treatment type
Synthetic Lab grown, identical appearance No astrological benefit

Frequently Asked Questions

How to identify real gemstones?

Always check certificates (IGI / GII / GRS / GIA / SGL) and consult an expert gemologist.

Are synthetic stones useful in astrology?

No, only natural stones carry planetary resonance.
