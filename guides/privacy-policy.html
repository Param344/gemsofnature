Privacy Policy

Last updated: Today

At Gems of Nature, your trust is our highest priority. We are fully committed to protecting your personal data and being 100% transparent about how we collect, use, store, and safeguard your information. This Privacy Policy explains your rights, our responsibilities, and the strict standards we follow to ensure your privacy is never compromised.

1. Information We Collect

We collect only the minimum information required to operate our services safely and effectively, including:

  • Identity Information: name, phone number, email address.
  • Order Information: billing/shipping addresses, items purchased, order history.
  • Technical Information: IP address, device/browser type, pages visited, usage patterns (for security & analytics).
  • Payment Information: processed securely through third-party gateways; we do not store card details on our servers.

We never collect unnecessary or sensitive personal information unless required by law.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your data is used strictly for legitimate business purposes, including:

  • Processing and delivering orders;
  • Providing customer support;
  • Sending essential updates about orders, deliveries or account activity;
  • Improving website functionality, performance and security;
  • Preventing fraud, abuse, and unauthorised access;
  • Sending offers or recommendations only with your consent.

We do not use your personal data for any purpose unrelated to your experience with Gems of Nature.

3. How We Protect Your Data

We apply industry-standard security measures to keep your information safe at all times, including:

  • SSL/TLS encryption across the entire website;
  • Firewall & Cloudflare protection for threat detection and prevention;
  • Encrypted servers and restricted data access;
  • Regular security audits and system monitoring;
  • No storage of financial information on our servers.

Despite our best efforts, no online transmission can be guaranteed 100% secure — but we take every possible step to minimise risk.

4. Sharing of Information

We never sell, rent, or trade your personal information — ever.

We only share information with trusted service partners who need it to perform essential tasks, such as:

  • Shipping/courier companies;
  • Payment processors (Razorpay, PayPal, Stripe, etc.);
  • Analytics and security tools.

Each partner is contractually required to keep your information confidential and secure, and they are prohibited from using it for their own purposes.

5. Your Privacy Rights

Depending on your location (including GDPR-supported regions), you may have the following rights:

  • Right to Access – request a copy of your personal data;
  • Right to Correction – update or fix your information;
  • Right to Deletion – request removal of your data;
  • Right to Restrict Processing;
  • Right to Opt-Out of Marketing;
  • Right to Withdraw Consent at any time.

To exercise any of these rights, email us at info@gemsofnature.shop. We will respond promptly and transparently.

6. Cookies & Tracking Technologies

We use cookies to:

  • Enable secure site functionality;
  • Maintain your shopping cart;
  • Improve performance and load times;
  • Understand how visitors use the site;
  • Personalise recommendations and offers.

You can disable cookies via your browser settings, though some features may not function correctly if cookies are disabled.

7. Data Retention

We retain personal data only for as long as necessary to:

  • Complete your orders;
  • Provide support;
  • Fulfil legal, accounting or tax obligations.

When data is no longer required, it is securely deleted or anonymised.

8. Children’s Privacy

Our services are not intended for children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect data from minors. If you believe a child has provided information, contact us immediately and we will remove it.

9. International Transfers

Your information may be processed or stored in countries other than your own. When we transfer data internationally, we take steps required by law to ensure an adequate level of protection for your data.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy to reflect legal requirements or changes in our practices. Any updates will be posted on this page with an updated “Last updated” date. We recommend checking this page periodically.

11. Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or wish to exercise your rights, please contact us at:

Email: info@gemsofnature.shop

Note: This policy is intended to be clear and practical. It does not create contractual rights beyond those established by applicable law.
