Privacy Policy

At Gems of Nature, we are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we collect, use and safeguard your personal information.

1. What Information We Collect

  • Contact information: name, email, phone number, shipping address.
  • Order information: items ordered, quantity, price, payment method.
  • Shipping details and tracking information.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To process orders, arrange shipping and handle returns or refunds.
  • To send order confirmation, shipping updates, and communications related to your purchase.
  • To improve our website, products and customer experience.

3. Data Security

We implement suitable technical and organizational measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, disclosure or misuse. Sensitive information is transmitted using secure protocols (HTTPS). We do not store your payment data on our servers — payments are processed by secure third-party gateways or manually after order confirmation.

4. Sharing & Disclosure

We do not sell, rent or share your personal data with third-party marketing companies. We may share limited data with our courier / logistics partners solely to fulfill shipping and delivery (name, address, phone). We may disclose information only when required by law or to protect our rights.

5. Cookies & Analytics

We use cookies and similar technologies to provide a better browsing and shopping experience. Cookies help us remember your preferences, keep you logged in and analyze website traffic. You can disable cookies in your browser but some features (cart, checkout) may not function properly.

6. Your Rights

  • You may request access, updates or deletion of your personal information by contacting us.
  • You can unsubscribe from promotional communications at any time.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy occasionally to reflect changes in laws, business practices or data handling. The updated version will be posted on this page with a revised “Last updated” date.
