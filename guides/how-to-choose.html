How to Choose a Gemstone

Learn how to pick the right stone based on astrology, energy and intention.

Start with Your Purpose

Choosing a gemstone depends on your goal. Different gemstones support different areas of life such as health, success, love, spiritual growth and protection.

  • Career / Business Growth: Yellow Sapphire, Emerald, Ruby
  • Health & Immunity: Red Coral, Pearl, Amethyst
  • Love & Marriage: Diamond, Opal, Rose Quartz
  • Calm & Peace: Blue Sapphire, Moonstone
  • Luck & Wealth: Cat’s Eye, Hessonite, Sunstone

Check Birth Chart (if required)

If you are selecting a gemstone for astrological use, get a proper Vedic astrology consultation.

Tip: Never wear Blue Sapphire, Hessonite (Gomed), or Cat’s Eye without consulting an astrologer — they are very strong and can backfire if wrong.

Clarity, Color, Cut & Carat (4Cs)

The quality of a gemstone is measured by:

Factor Why It Matters
Color The deeper the color, the more powerful and valuable the gemstone.
Clarity Fewer cracks/inclusions = better energy transmission.
Carat Astrological energy depends on weight — usually 5–7+ carats.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is expensive always better?

Not always — correct selection based on requirement is more important than price.

Can I wear multiple stones together?

Some stones complement each other, some clash. Consult an expert first.
