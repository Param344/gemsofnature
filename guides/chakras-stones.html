Chakras & Healing Stones

Discover how gemstones support your body’s 7 main energy centres for balance, clarity and well-being.

In yogic and tantra traditions, the human energy body contains 7 primary chakras aligned along the spine. Each chakra governs certain organs, emotions and life themes. When a chakra is weak or blocked, we may feel stuck, anxious, exhausted or disconnected.

Natural gemstones and crystals carry specific vibrations that resonate with each chakra. Wearing or meditating with the right stone can support healing, grounding and inner alignment.

How to Work with Chakra Stones

1. Wear on Body

Use pendants, bracelets, finger rings or malas. For example: Root stones near legs/feet, Heart stones as pendants, Throat stones as chokers.

2. Chakra Layout

Lie down comfortably and place one stone on each chakra area for 10–15 minutes. Breathe slowly and visualise coloured light at each centre.

3. Meditation & Japa

Hold a stone in your right hand or keep it on your altar while doing mantra japa, pranayama or meditation. Set a clear intention before starting.

Simple Daily Balancing Practice (5–10 minutes)

  1. Choose one chakra you wish to balance (for example Heart or Root).
  2. Hold the related stone or place it gently on that chakra area.
  3. Close your eyes and take 10 deep, slow breaths.
  4. With each inhale, imagine colour light of that chakra (red, orange, yellow, green, blue, indigo, violet) entering the body.
  5. With each exhale, release heaviness, fear or tension from that area into the earth.
  6. End with a short affirmation, for example: “I am safe and supported” (Root) or “My heart is open and protected” (Heart).

Tips & Precautions

Gentle vs Intense Stones

Stones like Amethyst, Rose Quartz, Clear Quartz, Aventurine are generally gentle. Very intense stones such as Blue Sapphire, Cat’s Eye, Hessonite or Moldavite should be used carefully and preferably under guidance.

Combine with Care

For chakra healing it is fine to use multiple crystals together. For pure astrological gemstones (e.g. Blue Sapphire, Ruby), always get a personalised recommendation before mixing with other powerful stones.

Note: Chakra stones support your energy body and emotional balance. They are not a replacement for medical or psychological treatment. Always consult your doctor for health conditions.
